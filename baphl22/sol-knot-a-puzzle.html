Knot a Puzzle Solution

by Ata Gurpinar

The rope segments can be rearranged like a jigsaw puzzle and placed in the positions on the jumprope to form the following diagram:

As hinted by the flavor text mentioning "eleven-year-olds" and the eleven segments on each jump rope handle, the answer to each clue is eleven letters long, and they have been provided in alphabetical order by answer:

  • Uncontrolled driving on top of, not through, water on the road. - AQUAPLANING
  • Pageant winner (2 wds.) - BEAUTY QUEEN
  • Instruct through performative example - DEMONSTRATE
  • Having two colors - DICHROMATIC
  • Final part of a journey or project - HOMESTRETCH
  • Yellow condiment component - MUSTARD SEED
  • Incessant, like a "story" with a luck dragon (Hyph.) - NEVER-ENDING
  • Like a medical treatment that doesn't need a scalpel - NONSURGICAL
  • Visual similarity - RESEMBLANCE
  • Approve a document, often without inspection - RUBBERSTAMP
  • Measurements for sound or colors - WAVELENGTH
