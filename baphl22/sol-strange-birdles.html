Each puzzle answer in this round has a 4 letter word, a 5-letter word, and a 6-letter word (in a different order):

  • 4-5-6: WIND CHILL FACTOR
  • 4-6-5: JOHN PHILIP SOUSA
  • 5-4-6: LOOSE LEAF BINDER
  • 5-6-4: MONEY MARKET FUND
  • 6-5-4: BOSTON METRO WEST

The combination 6-4-5 is not present in the puzzle answers, but the rows of turkeys match this order in their numbers of feathers.

Each row of Turkeys can be assigned the words with length equal to the number of feathers on that row, in alphabetical order. If solvers do not have all puzzle answers (or even if they do), the assignment of word-to-turkey can be determined with logic. In the easier version of the puzzles, the three turkeys from the same puzzle answer share a pattern on their body.

Each row of Turkeys represent a game of Wordle, as hinted by the "popular games" mentioned in the flavor text and the yellow and green feathers.

Order   6-Letter Word   4-Letter Word   5-Letter Word
1   BINDER   FUND   CHILL
2   BOSTON   JOHN   LOOSE
3   FACTOR   LEAF   METRO
4   MARKET   WEST   MONEY
5   PHILIP   WIND   SOUSA
Answer   PARDON   FOWL   MOUTH

Although the 4-letter words can logically be solved with FOLW or FOWL, only one is a word. Likewise for the 5-letter words, only one of MOUTH and MOUHT is a word.

So reading down the three games of Wordle, the solution is PARDON FOWL MOUTH.
